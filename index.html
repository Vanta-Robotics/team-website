WHO WE ARE

Based in the vibrant tech hub of Orlando, Florida, Vanta Robotics is a dedicated FIRST Tech Challenge team fueled by a passion for innovation and problem-solving. We are a collective of students, mentors, and community members committed to pushing the boundaries of technology through collaboration, creativity, and competition. Our mission is to inspire the next generation of STEM leaders while building advanced, competitive robots.
